Resume Upload & Management

ShortlistPro supports multiple file formats and automatically extracts key information from resumes.

Supported Formats

  • PDF documents
  • Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
  • Plain text files

Automatic Data Extraction

Our AI automatically extracts:

  • Contact information
  • Work experience
  • Education background
  • Skills and certifications
  • Professional summary

Creating Job Descriptions

Create detailed job descriptions that help our AI match the best candidates to your positions.

Best Practices

  • • Be specific about required skills and experience
  • • Include company culture and values
  • • Specify education requirements clearly
  • • Mention location and remote work policies

AI-Powered Matching

Our advanced AI analyzes resumes and job descriptions to provide accurate candidate matching scores.

Matching Criteria

  • Skills alignment
  • Experience level
  • Education background
  • Industry experience
  • Career progression
